Contact the vim-latex-devel team

Once upon a time, vim-latex-devel@lists.sourceforge.net was an open list which did not restrict unsubscribed people from posting. Unfortunately, we got too much spam and have decided to make it a closed list. This means that you can only e-mail vim-latex-devel if you are subscribed to it.

You can contact the list in a number of ways:

  • If you just to shoot off a quick e-mail, then continue onto the form below. Fill in the required information and thats it. E-mail will be sent to you and to the vim-latex-devel group on your behalf. There is no guarantee that the person who replies will include you in the the reply although it is usual practice.
  • You can also submit a bug report using the vim-latex project bug tracker. Please do this while logged in if possible.
  • Finally, you can subscribe to the list. Subscribing is easy. Just fill in your e-mail address at this page. You will be asked to confirm. vim-latex-devel is moderately active list which gets about 3-4 mails per day on average.
