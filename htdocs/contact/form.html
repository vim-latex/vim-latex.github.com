Contact the vim-latex-devel team

Once upon a time, vim-latex-devel@lists.sourceforge.net was an open list which did not restrict unsubscribed people from posting. Unfortunately, we got too much spam and have decided to make it a closed list. This means that you can only e-mail vim-latex-devel if you are subscribed to it. If you wish to subscribe, go to this page. vim-latex-devel is moderately active list which gets about 3-4 mails per day on average.
